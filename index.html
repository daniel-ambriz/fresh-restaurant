About Us

Chef Tony is a Denver native with a background in all-natural foods. He owns a farm in Boulder, where he raises deer, bison, and chickens. You can rest assured that everything you get on your plate was grown or raised less than 2 hours away!

As a graduate from the Art Institute of Michigan, Chef Tony would like the world to know that being a vegetarian does not mean that everything has to taste green, and also that being a carnivore doesn't mean that you won't be hooked on juicy veggie burgers from Fresh Restaurant!
